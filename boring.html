what is this?

this is what it looks like without the stylesheet.

A simple opinionated classless stylesheet to make default html look better. There aren't any special classes or ids, just straight semantic html. Load the stylesheet and add your content.

This is a good, quick stylesheet to throw on top of simple html to get a decent looking presentation. You aren't going to make overly complex websites with this, but still better than what the browser gives you.

This content is in a section so it works for making different styled cards.
